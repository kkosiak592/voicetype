## Decisions Made

- FftPlanner is created once before the async spawn, and the resulting `Arc<dyn Fft<f32>>` is moved into the async block. This avoids per-tick replanning (a known performance pitfall documented in RESEARCH.md).
- RMS gate set at 0.02 threshold — below this, all bins are zeroed to prevent ambient mic noise from triggering waveform movement at rest.
- Three bloom passes use `globalCompositeOperation: "lighter"` (additive blending), which creates the characteristic neon halo by accumulating color intensity per pixel.
- Bell curve envelope pre-computed as `BELL_16` module-level constant — same gaussian shape as the old FrequencyBars, centers the waveform amplitude peak.
- Bottom mirrored waveform drawn at 60% opacity relative to top — makes the thin 30px canvas look fuller without dominating over the top curves.
- `devicePixelRatio` scaling applied once in the mount useEffect (sets canvas.width/height and ctx.scale) — not per frame.
