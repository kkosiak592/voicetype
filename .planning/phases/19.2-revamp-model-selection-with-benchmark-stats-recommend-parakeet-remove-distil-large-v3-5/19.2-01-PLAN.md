<objective>
Remove distil-large-v3.5 from all backend and frontend model surfaces, embed benchmark stats in model descriptions, and make parakeet-tdt-v2-fp32 the universal recommended model.

Purpose: Benchmark data (Quick tasks 27-37) shows distil-large-v3.5 is the worst performer (15.6s avg latency, 5.9% WER, 513 MB) with no advantage over any other model. Parakeet has the best accuracy (5.3% WER) at reasonable latency and runs on all hardware. This cleanup removes a misleading option and gives users data-driven model selection.

Output: Clean model registry with 4 models (large-v3-turbo, small-en, parakeet, moonshine-tiny), benchmark stats in descriptions, single universal recommendation.
</objective>
