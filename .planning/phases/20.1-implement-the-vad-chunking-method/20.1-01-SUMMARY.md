- Audio <=30s bypasses chunking entirely for all engines (zero overhead)

## Files Created/Modified

- `src-tauri/src/pipeline.rs` - Added VAD chunk dispatch to Whisper and Parakeet engine arms before spawn_blocking
- `src-tauri/src/transcribe_moonshine.rs` - Updated to call `vad_chunk_audio` directly instead of `vad_chunk_for_moonshine`

## Decisions Made
- `vad_chunk_for_moonshine` kept as thin wrapper for backward compatibility (though no callers remain)
- VAD chunking computed BEFORE `spawn_blocking` to avoid ONNX Runtime issues inside blocking context
- `CHUNK_THRESHOLD_SAMPLES` defined locally in each engine arm rather than shared constant
