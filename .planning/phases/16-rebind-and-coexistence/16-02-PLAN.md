<objective>
Surface hook installation failure in the settings UI as an inline warning below the hotkey field. The frontend queries hook availability once on mount and passes it as a prop to GeneralSection, which conditionally renders the warning.

Purpose: INT-03 requires that hook failure is visible to the user. Without this, the user sees ctrl+shift+space as their hotkey with no explanation of why their modifier-only combo was replaced.
Output: App.tsx loads hookAvailable via IPC, GeneralSection.tsx renders inline warning when hook is unavailable.
</objective>
